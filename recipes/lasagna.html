Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

This lasagna is a classic comfort food made with layers of pasta, sauce, and cheese. It is filling, easy to make, and great for family meals.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the chicken pieces until golden brown.
  3. Prepare the lasagna layers with pasta, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes.
  5. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
